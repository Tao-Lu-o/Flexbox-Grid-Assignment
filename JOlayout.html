Grounded Ministries is a Christian ministry focused on teaching and practicing Bible-centered apologetics. What this means is that we primarily rely upon on the Word to provide us with the basis to break down intellectual and emotional barriers to the Gospel. Other arguments made from a scientific, philosophical, or otherwise secular basis are extremely useful and we teach them in addition, but we give preferential treatment to arguments made directly from the Bible.

To us, the practice of apologetics is one of the essential parts of evangelism, but our founders thought there wasn't enough deference given to the faith which we all share. As Hebrews 4:12 says, "the word of God is living and active, sharper than any two-edged sword, piercing to the division of soul and of spirit, of joints and of marrow, and discerning the thoughts and intentions of the heart" (ESV). What better resource do we have than the Word to break down the barriers of sinfulness in man's mind and open their heart to the divine surgeon? If anything should take the forefront of apologetics, it should be God's Holy, Inspired, Inerrant, and Infallible Word.

Do you agree with our approach to apologetics? If you do, please sign up for our monthly newsletter! We catalog major events in the world and the universal church, where our wonderful columnists here at Grounded Ministries analyze and review them. We also provide the latest scoops on what members just like you are doing all around the world in hopes that it will spur you onto action, encouraging you as you live your life radically for Christ!
